Flight Analysis for consulting hubs in the US

Made: 01/10/20

With the idea of expanding our operations to the United States the consulting company is planning to open several offices due to growth opportunities in the region. One of the biggest cost factors in consulting is man hours. The goal is to ensure that we have a cost-efficient business centre and optimal work environment for our consultants. With this goal in mind, we must maximise efficiency through travel whilst minimising delay and lost revenue. Therefore, the current analysis made in PowerBI will help the C-levels in choosing which cities to open our new offices.

Air Quality Index

Project Earth was borned to increase transparency of pollution levels in various cities within South Korea. With this idea in mind, I built an end-to-end data pipeline to provide real-time information of pollution levels and ensure instant notifications of any sudden spikes to ensure timely action plans. Blockchain venture capital network analysis

Made: 14/01/21

The analysis showcases data from 2015-2020. Different graphs were built (geographical, industry type, company) in order to highlight key aspects around blockchain and start-up investments. As trade moves more and more over the digital landscape, there will be a need for a reliable and secure way of tracking transactions, logistics, patient health data and many other kinds of sensitive information. The startup and venture capital ecosystems have realized this and are thus looking to capitalize on what many are seeing as one of the biggest innovations of the decade: blockchains.
